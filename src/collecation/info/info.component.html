About Us

The Community Waste Reporting App empowers citizens to actively participate in maintaining a clean and sustainable environment. By reporting illegal dumping and overflowing bins, users contribute to healthier communities, protect wildlife, and foster a sense of collective responsibility.

Easy Reporting Snap photos and provide details with just a few taps.
Real-Time Updates Track the status of your reports and see community impact.
Collaborative Effort Join thousands of users making a difference together.
Environmental Impact Help reduce pollution and protect local ecosystems.
